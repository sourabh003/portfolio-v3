About Me

I am a Full-stack developer with 6+ years of experience building fast, scalable, and user-focused web applications. I work mainly with React, Next.js, TypeScript, and Node.js, and enjoy creating clean, performant frontend systems backed by solid APIs and databases.

Skilled in modern frontend architecture, state management, SSR/SSG patterns, and building reusable UI layers. I am comfortable across the backend as well, working with Prisma, SQL, and REST-based services.

Beyond the code, I bring experience in mentoring teammates, improving code quality, and contributing to system-level decisions that make products easier to maintain and scale. I'm always learning, improving my problem-solving skills, and exploring better ways to build reliable web experiences.
